============================
Boss' abilities plugin
"rage_overlay"
============================

rage_overlay - Temporarily block players' screens with an overlay
	0 - slot of ability (def.0)
		-1 - When Boss lost life (if he has over 1)
		>>0 - Rage<<
		1 - Brave Jump
		2 - Demopan's charge of targe, projectiles etc
		3 - Weighdown
		4...7 - Not used.
	1 - path to overlay ("root" is \tf\materials\)
	2 - duration (def.6)
