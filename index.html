Welcome to the official FF2 webpage!

Freak Fortress 2 is a one-versus-all plugin designed for the popular game Team Fortress 2. Developed as a more customizable version of Versus Saxton Hale, its precursor, FF2 allows anyone to easily create their own bosses.

Want the latest version? Check out the Releases page. For those who always want the latest bleeding-edge features (and bugs), development builds are available on Jenkins. You can also check out the forums to learn more. Brought to you by 50DKP.
